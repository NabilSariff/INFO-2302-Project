ST MARTIN ISLAND Saint Martin is an island in the northeast Carribean Sea, approximately 300km east of Puerto Rico. The 87-square-kilometer island is divided roughly between the French Republic and the Kingdom of the Netherlands, but the two parts are roughly equal in population. The main cities are Philipsburg on the Dutch side and Marigot on the French side. The Dutch side is more heavily populated. The largest settlement on the entire island is Lower Prince's Quarter, which is on the Dutch side.
